Our Departments

Cardioology

The Cardiology Department has gained reputation for its dynamic and innovation in the diagnosis, investigation and treatment of patients with all forms of heart diseases, both generally encountered and complex cases. We have a dedicated unit of four full-time Consultants, and a team of junior doctors, nurses, and technicians trained to the highest standard in cardiac assessment.

Gastroenterology

Department of Gastroenterology & Hepatology is devoted to the clinical care of patients with gastrointestinal and liver disorders. The department is home to excellent patient care, and our specialists employ the most advanced diagnostic and therapeutic endoscopy services to ensure the best outcomes for each patient.

Medical Lab

Lab Medicine Department has become the leading diagnostic laboratory in Bangladesh by virtue of its uniqueness in offering diverse tests, reliability and prompt turn around time. We provide state-of-the-art reliable diagnostic services 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for patients, general practitioners and other hospitals in the country.

Dental

Our dental practice is highly specialised and covers oral and maxillofacial surgery, oral surgery, orthodontics, paediatric dentistry, and restorative dentistry.

Paediatric Surgery

Paediatric Surgery is different from other surgical principles as it deals with children, who are special patients because of their potential to grow, anatomic variations, different disease patterns, and different psychology.

Neurology

The Neurology Department has experienced American Board Certified Neurologist and also the only qualified epilepsy specialist of the country. The department uses the latest technology and facilities, and is staffed by an experienced team of neurologists, nurses and technologists, who are committed to patient care.

Orthopaedics

The Department of Orthopaedics has three full-time Consultants, one part-time Consultant, a number of junior doctors, and supporting paramedical staff trained to deliver a 100% success rate. Our team diagnoses, operates and treats problems affecting the bones, joints and muscles of patients encompassing all age groups.

Neonatology

Department of Neonatology provides care for infants born in our Maternity Unit, as well as more advanced care and total management of infants suffering from complex medical problems through the hospital’s Neonatal ICU.

Internal Medicine

Department of Internal Medicine focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of non-surgical conditions in adults. The department provides a wide range of high quality cross-specialty health care services, including physical examinations, preventive screenings and immunizations, urgent care for medical conditions, and referrals to surgical specialists when appropriate.
